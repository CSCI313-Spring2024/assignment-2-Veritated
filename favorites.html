My Favorite Things

Websites

One of my favorite things to do in my spare time is to play DND with my friends. This makes Wikidot one of my favorite sites as it has resources and is much more intuitive and easy to use than the physical copy of the player's handbook is.

Along these lines, I also regularily use Roll20 to run these games so our DM can give us a better idea of what is happening especially spatially as most of my group does not have great imaginations when it comes to imagining rooms and remembering where people are in the room. Although this is not a great site (it is not intuitive, especially for a DM) it is the best option we have found and it is free to use.

Another thing I greatly enjoy is music (as evidenced by my minor) and as such I really enjoy IMSLP which is a free library of music. I often get and print off my solo repetoire from there. It has a wide variety of pieces and still avoids copywrite by only having the music of composers that have been dead for at least 70 years. This means that I can find just about any music from the Romantic period and prior, although contemporary music is currently harder to find. It also saves me money since I don't have to buy every piece of music I want to learn.
